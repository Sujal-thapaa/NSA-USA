How Our Community Fund Works

Simple, transparent, and effective community support

1

Community Donates

Each member contributes $1 to build our emergency fund. With 10,000-50,000 members, we create a powerful safety net.

2

Emergency Occurs

When a community member faces death, accident, or severe hardship, they or their family can request help.

3

Review & Verify

Our volunteer committee reviews requests to ensure legitimacy and urgent need for community support.

4

Funds Released

Approved requests receive immediate financial assistance from our community fund to help during difficult times.
