Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about NSA Network

How do I add my NSA chapter to the network?

You can add your NSA chapter by clicking the "Add NSA" button in the navigation or using the submission form. We'll review your submission and add it to our database within 48 hours.

How can I submit an event to the Events Hub?

Visit the Events Hub page and click "Submit Event" to add your competition, cultural night, or other community event. Include all relevant details and we'll feature it on our platform.

Is NSA Network free to use?

Yes! NSA Network is completely free for all users. We're committed to serving the Nepali student community without any cost barriers.

How do I update information for my NSA chapter?

Send us an email at contact@nsanetwork.org with the updated information for your NSA chapter. Include the university name and the changes you'd like to make.

Can I volunteer for NSA Network?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers to help expand our network. Contact us to learn about volunteer opportunities in your area.

How accurate is the information on the platform?

We strive to maintain accurate and up-to-date information. All submissions are reviewed, and we regularly verify contact details and social media links.
